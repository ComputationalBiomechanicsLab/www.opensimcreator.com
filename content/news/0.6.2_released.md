0.6.2 adds support for order-independent transparency rendering, tooling
and visualization for the new (experimental) OpenSim `Scholz2015GeometryPath`,
and MSI/portable windows installers, which makes it easier to deploy to
many machines.

Pictured: `Scholz2015GeometryPathExample.osim`, included as an example model
with this release.
